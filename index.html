Our Story

Our restaurant began on April 26th, 2002 in San Francisco. Focusing on the goals of “Quality, Healthy, and Tradition” the company is family operation making each dish in our own kitchens from scratch.

Through hard work and dedication, Gusto Restaurant began building its brand around the idea of delicious and authentic traditional food.
